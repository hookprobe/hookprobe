Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with HookProbe?

Check out our Quick Install Guide. It takes about 15-20 minutes to get HookProbe running on your hardware.

Is HookProbe really free?

Yes! HookProbe is 100% free and open-source under the MIT License. You can use it for personal or commercial purposes without any fees.

What hardware do I need?

HookProbe runs on a wide range of hardware, from Raspberry Pi 4 (8GB RAM) to enterprise servers. See our Hardware Compatibility Guide for details.

Do you offer commercial support?

We're working on commercial support options for enterprises and MSSPs. Email enterprise@hookprobe.com to express your interest and we'll notify you when it's available.

How can I contribute to HookProbe?

We welcome contributions! Check out our Contributing Guide to get started. You can contribute code, documentation, testing, or financial support via GitHub Sponsors.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! HookProbe is designed with privacy in mind. All your data stays on your devices. We're GDPR compliant and never collect or share your personal information. Learn more in our Privacy Policy.

Where We're Based

HookProbe is a distributed open-source project with contributors from around the world. Our core team is based in:

  • Europe: Bucharest, Romania
  • North America: Various locations
  • Asia Pacific: Various locations

We operate 24/7 thanks to our global community of contributors and maintainers.
