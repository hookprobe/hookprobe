    About Remote Access

    Access your Fortress from anywhere!

    Remote Access uses Cloudflare Tunnels to securely expose your Fortress dashboard to the internet. This allows you to:

    • Check security status from your phone
    • Manage your network while traveling
    • Receive and respond to alerts remotely
    • Grant secure access to IT support
    Secure by Design

    Cloudflare Tunnels create an outbound-only connection from your network. No ports are opened on your firewall, and all traffic is encrypted.
